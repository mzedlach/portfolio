About the Project

Toggle Book Factory creates highly customized books for children in order to empower children, encourage reading, and invigorate learning. Book purchases are available through Shopify.

Technology

I created customized templates for products and pages that provided solutions to emerging business needs and unexpected issues. Customized product templates enabled additional user input that was later used in book creation. Customized page templates allowed for a more unique and visually appealing display of information. Various apps were integrated for site tracking, email, product reviews, and site backups. Frequent collaboration and follow up with the owner allowed for smoother operations and realization of solutions for unique challenges. Technologies used:
  • CSS
  • HTML
  • Liquid
  • Javascript
  • Shopify admin pages
  • Github
